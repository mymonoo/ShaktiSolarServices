PM KUSUM YOJNA

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan

Empowering Farmers with Subsidies

The PM-KUSUM scheme aims to provide energy security to farmers in India, along with honoring Indiaâ€™s commitment to increase the share of installed capacity of electric power from non-fossil-fuel sources to 40% by 2030.


Major Benefits:

  • Up to 60-90% subsidy on solar pumps.
  • Reduced dependency on diesel and grid electricity.
  • Opportunity to sell excess power to the grid.
  • Environment-friendly irrigation.
